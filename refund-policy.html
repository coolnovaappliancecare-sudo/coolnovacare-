Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Overview

CoolNova Care aims to provide reliable and transparent appliance repair services. This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which refunds may be considered.

2. Inspection Charges

Inspection or visiting charges, where applicable, are generally non-refundable after the technician has visited the service location and completed inspection or diagnosis.

3. Repair Service Charges

Payments made for completed and accepted repair services are generally non-refundable unless required by applicable law or covered under a valid service commitment.

4. Spare Parts Charges

Charges for spare parts that have been installed or specially ordered for a customer are generally non-refundable unless the part is defective and covered under applicable warranty terms.

5. Advance Payments

Advance payments made for special-order parts or confirmed repair work may not be refundable after the procurement process has started.

6. Duplicate Payments

If a duplicate payment is made accidentally, the extra amount will be verified and refunded after confirmation.

7. Cancelled Appointments

If a customer cancels a booking before service begins, refund eligibility will depend on whether any inspection, travel, or preparation costs have already been incurred.

8. Failed Repairs

If an appliance cannot be repaired due to unavailable parts, discontinued models, severe damage, or technical limitations, refund decisions will be evaluated individually based on completed work and applicable charges.

9. Warranty Related Requests

Eligible warranty concerns will be inspected and handled according to warranty terms. Warranty service does not automatically qualify for a monetary refund.

10. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Completed repair services accepted by the customer.
  • Damage caused by misuse or unauthorized repair attempts.
  • Normal wear and tear.
  • Issues unrelated to the original service request.
  • Customer refusal after approved repair completion.

11. Refund Request Process

To request a refund review, customers should provide:

  • Customer name
  • Registered mobile number
  • Invoice or receipt details
  • Date of service
  • Reason for refund request
  • Supporting information if required

12. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method or another agreed method. Processing time may vary depending on banks or payment providers.

13. Payment Disputes

Customers should contact CoolNova Care directly for payment-related concerns before initiating external disputes so that we can review and resolve the matter.

14. Policy Updates

CoolNova Care may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes become effective after publication on this page.
